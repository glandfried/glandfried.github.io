Gustavo Landfried

Computer Science, Anthropological Science.

Artificial Intelligence Lab

High Performance Computing Lab

Universidad de Buenos Aires

I am developing the Computational Social Science area in one of the main scientific centers of Latin America. As a Social Anthropologist, I am currently finishing a PhD in Computer Science. My work is focused on studying how people learn in cultural evolution contexts, exploiting the virtually infinite repository of human behavior available in Internet. Additionally, I am leading a large group of young anthropologists, who have already begun to train in mathematics and computational science. With patience and perseverance, we are laying the foundations of a future interdisciplinary social science research center in Latin America.
