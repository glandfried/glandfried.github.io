Artificial cultural evolution algorithm

Jan 1, 2019

Humans had an adaptive leap when they developed the ability to imitate, to acquire information based on the experience of others. This allowed learning and memory decentralization in a population of agents, leading to a process of cultural evolution in which valuable knowledge emerges even if it is too costly for it to be discovered by individual agents. We are working on a social reinforcement learning model, based on individual experience and social learning transmition, in order to study non-analytical properties of the cultural evolution process.

Gustavo Landfried
Computer Science, Anthropological Science.
