As a Social Anthropologist, I am currently finishing a PhD in Computer Science. I study how people learn in cultural evolution contexts applying Bayesian inference rooted on causal models. With several colleagues, we are working to lay the foundations of an interdisciplinary social sciences research center in Latin America. I just hope that our work will serve the historical project that reveres life over profitability.
