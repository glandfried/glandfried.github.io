#I believe that a good social science will provide us with the answers to fundamental problems that life on earth faces in this historical colonial-modern stage. With several colleagues, we are working hard to lay the foundations of an interdisciplinary social sciences research center in Latin America. As a Social Anthropologist, I am currently finishing a PhD in Computer Science, developing the Computational Social Science area in one of the main scientific centers of the southern hemisphere. Additionally, I am leading a large group of young anthropologists, who have already begun to train in mathematics and computational science. I just hope that our work will serve the historical project that reveres life over profitability.
